AI-Driven Marketplace Operations & GTM Localization

Automating regulatory compliance, product localization, and catalog operations for international expansion.

The Strategic Intent

  • The Operational Bottleneck: Expanding health, beauty, and consumer product catalogs into international marketplaces requires strict regulatory compliance checks (country-specific dosage limits, mandatory warning texts, and Clean Label ingredient restrictions). Manual cross-border verification severely slows down time-to-market.
  • The AI & No-Code Solution: Built an end-to-end automated GTM localization and compliance engine for Lumina Marketplace connecting web requests, Make webhooks, Anthropic Claude, and Google Sheets.
  • Proof of Capability: Demonstrates complete operational fluency: connecting browser-based web applications to Make automation, evaluating complex regulatory guardrails (e.g., Italian Ministero della Salute, French ANSES, German BfR), and delivering fully translated, compliant product listings and marketing copy at scale.
